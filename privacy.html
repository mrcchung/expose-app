Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

Exposé ("we", "our", or "us") is a photo strip app made by Chris Chung. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Exposé collects minimal information to provide a great experience:

  • Camera and Photos: The app uses your iPhone camera to take photos and may save strips to your photo library. Photos are processed entirely on-device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to Exposé Pro, Apple processes your payment. We receive only a receipt token confirming your subscription status — never credit card details.
  • AI Stylization Credits: When you use AI stylization features, the image is sent to a secure third-party AI service (Replicate) solely to generate the stylized result. It is not stored or used for training.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymized crash logs may be sent to Apple via standard iOS crash reporting to help us fix bugs.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not collect your precise location
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites
  • Your photos stay on your device — we never upload them to our servers

Subscriptions

Exposé Pro is offered as an auto-renewable subscription through Apple's App Store. Subscription management (including cancellations) is handled through your Apple ID settings. We do not have access to your payment details.

Children's Privacy

Exposé is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is nothing to retain or delete on our end. Photos, frames, and settings are stored locally on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Exposé uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — for payment processing and subscription management
  • Replicate — for AI stylization features (images sent are not stored)
  • Apple CloudKit — for syncing app data (future feature; frames and preferences only)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Contact Us
